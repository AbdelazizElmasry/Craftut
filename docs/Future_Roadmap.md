# Future Roadmap

This roadmap represents current priorities and intended directions. It is not a commitment or guaranteed delivery schedule. Plans are subject to change based on resource availability, technical findings, and market validation.

---

## Short-Term (Current Focus)

Priority: Resolve known mechanical limitations and complete core workflows.

- Improve structural rigidity
- Reduce vibration through frame reinforcement or damping
- Increase reliability through systematic testing and iteration
- Complete USB model loading workflow
- Continue validation testing with structured test plans

## Long-Term (Strategic Direction)

Priority: Build the educational platform and ecosystem.

- Educational platform with structured learning content
- Courses and guided learning pathways
- Community features (sharing, remixing, collaboration)
- Challenges and gamification elements
- Expanded STEM ecosystem (project kits, sensors, curriculum modules)

---

## Uncertainty and Risk

Future plans are presented with the following uncertainties:

- **Technical risk:** Structural improvements may require significant mechanical redesign, impacting timeline and cost.
- **Resource risk:** Platform features (cloud, mobile, content) require dedicated software teams beyond current hardware-focused resourcing.
- **Market risk:** Educational market adoption depends on pricing, distribution, and curriculum alignment not yet validated.
- **Regulatory risk:** Child safety products require certification processes (e.g., electrical safety, material safety) that have not been initiated.

These risks are acknowledged and will inform prioritization decisions.
